How to play
  • Players take turns clicking between two adjacent dots to draw a line (horizontal or vertical).
  • When you complete the fourth side of a box, you claim it with your initial (A or B).
  • Completing a box gives you an immediate extra turn.
  • When no lines remain, the player with more boxes wins.
